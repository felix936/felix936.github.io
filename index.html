My internship at Bosch was an exciting and enriching experience, during which I was able to contribute my own ideas and gain valuable insights into electrical engineering.

My internship at the University Hospital Freiburg was a great opportunity to broaden my horizons, acquire valuable knowledge and improve my social skills.
